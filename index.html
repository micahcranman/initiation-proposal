I

The problem

Beyond the facilitator network, there's no real system to orient clients, hold an ongoing community, support preparation and integration, and — most importantly — upsell them on facilitation.

Clients need to understand why facilitation matters and what skills a dark retreat demands. Building that internally means staff and ongoing management Acraya can't justify right now. This is the most elegant way to solve it.

II

The solution

Free · always included

A short "mini-course" — the map of the territory — orienting every client to the dark retreat and the skills to navigate it safely.

Optional · paid

Initiation — a self-study + group-based preparation & integration program. It's the natural upsell path to facilitation ("I need more preparation"), or a budget-friendly route for clients who won't book 1:1.

The whole framework is non-denominational and non-dogmatic — built around the dynamics a client actually experiences in the dark, compatible with any lineage or method rather than competing with one.

III

What Initiation is

Over the past several months we've built a full curriculum around the dark retreat, expressed as an initiation process — a hero's journey by ship. You set out to seek treasure, only to discover the transformation itself is the treasure.

Each leg of the voyage maps onto real inner work — the crew is the parts of us that surface, the crow's nest is awareness practice, and the cargo is the beliefs and identity we carry. The journey is the container; the change that happens inside it is the point.

A client who buys it gets a self-study online course — the map of what happens psychologically in darkness, how to work with the parts of themselves that surface, and a concrete protocol for regulating through intense moments in the dark — plus weekly live group calls, before the retreat for preparation and after it for integration. We run these ourselves to start.

This isn't theoretical, and we're not learning on the job. The path and the metaphor are ours to build, but the tools and the approach are fundamentally Severin's — refined across thousands of hours of guiding real people through the dark. We're taking an experience that already works and packaging it for Acraya's context: the same frameworks we're bringing into the study, delivered in a form optimized for your clients.
